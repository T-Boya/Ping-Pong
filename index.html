Ping Pong!

Let's play ping-pong, Moring style!

How to play:
Write a number in the box on bottom left hand side of the page. All the numbers between 0 and that number will be printed out. Boring right? NOT! Numbers divisible by 3 are replaced with "ping",
numbers divisibe by 5 are replaced with "pong",
best of all, numbers divisible by 15 are replaced with "pingpong"
